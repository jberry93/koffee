Koffee Styleguide

A brief overview of the fonts and colors used for the Koffee site

Foundation

Grid

Koffee utilizes Bootstrap's 12-column grid. There are 10 pixels of padding between each grid element.

Color Palette

white #FFFFFF

black #000000

brown #3e2723

Koffee sticks to clean black and white fonts but a dark brown is used to connect back to the product

Font
